02 — EXTERIOR

Form follows the airflow.

A low-slung monocoque shell, sculpted around the cabin. The camera circles the body as the studio key-light rakes across the panels — every crease there to move air, not to decorate.

03 — POWERTRAIN

The heart, taken apart.

The shell lifts away and the drivetrain explodes into its parts — block, intakes, wheels and rotors — each one labelled as it floats free, then drawn back into place.

04 — CLEARCOAT

Scroll sweeps the dial. The clearcoat re-tints in real time and the studio's rim-light follows the hue — the whole room wears the colour with the car.
